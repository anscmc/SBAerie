Other Services


Small Business Alternatives offers financial planning services for small businesses in Erie, PA. We understand how overwhelming financial planning for small businesses can be, especially if you are a start-up with no prior business management experience.

Financial planning services that we offer include:

  • Quick Book tutorials and set-up
  • New business set-up including obtaining an EIN number and PA 100
  • Benchmarking
  • Budgets, forecasts, and projections
  • Tax planning and consulting
    •

    From Quick Book tutorials and set up to budgeting and forecasting, our professional accounting staff can help with all of your small business financial planning needs. As a small business in Erie, PA, it's important to properly plan your finances well into the future. Our certified accountants will consult with you to determine your financial goals and help you reach them.
