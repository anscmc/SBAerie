Accounting & Bookkeeping


Small Business Alternatives provides small business accounting and bookkeeping services for small businesses in Erie, PA. We don't just do basic accounting at our office, although there is a lot of it. When it comes to accounting and bookkeeping services, our professional accounting staff can do everything from A to Z and everything in between. We can help you with everything from Quick Books support to monthly, quarterly, and semi-annual sales tax filing.

When bringing papers in, SBA will need:

(*If at all possible, please drop off on a quarterly basis*)

  • Receipts, neat and orderly
  • Bank statements
  • Check registers
  • Sales tax information, if applicable
  • Payroll information, if applicable
    •
    • Quarterly filings
    • W2's

    We've had plenty of clients bring us bags of receipts and say: "Help me! I own a business and I know nothing about how to keep the books in order and even less about doing business taxes." We take the bags, give them a hug, and say "No Problem. This is why we are here!". When working with Small Business Alternatives, you can ensure to lean on our professional accounting staff for all of your small business accounting and bookkeeping needs.
