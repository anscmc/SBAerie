Taxes


Small Business Alternatives offers tax accounting services for small businesses in Erie, PA. Professional tax accounting services require communication between clients and tax consultants. We integrate accounting, management consulting, and clients' future plans to manage taxes and effectively plan for your future needs. We offer small business tax accounting services such as personal, partnerships, corporate, and not-for-profit tax return preparation as well as tax planning and consulting.

Minimization of tax obligations is achieved by our knowledge of the tax regulations, constant monitoring, training in tax laws and regulations, and integration of tax accounting services. By working with the tax accountants at Small Business Alternatives, you can rest assured knowing that all of your taxes are being handled properly.
