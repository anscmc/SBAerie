Payroll


Small Business Alternatives offers professional payroll services for small businesses in Erie, PA. We are able to process payroll on a weekly, bi-weekly, or bi-monthly basis. Our professional accounting staff will work with you to determine the best payroll services that fit your needs. Our payroll fees will vary depending on the number of employees, services needed, frequency of payroll, etc. We recommend using direct deposit, but it is not necessary.

Process Payroll Fees/ Quarterly Taxes:

  • 1st Quarter Payroll Taxes - Due April 30, 2018
  • 2nd Quarter Payroll Taxes - Due July 31, 2018
  • 3rd Quarter Payroll Taxes - Due October 31, 2018
  • 4th Quarter Payroll Taxes - Due January 31, 2020
